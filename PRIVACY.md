# Privacy Policy for FlowFI
### Last Updated: June 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how data is handled by the FlowFI desktop application.

## 1. Data Collection and Processing
FlowFI is a strictly local, open-source application. We do not collect, store, harvest, or transmit any personal information, telemetry, or usage metrics.

All operations, including image processing, bespoke parameter design, and feature selection, are executed entirely locally on your device. Your analytical data, .tiff images, and tabular files never leave your machine.

## 2. Third-Party Services
FlowFI does not integrate with any third-party tracking software, analytics services, or advertising frameworks.

## 3. Changes to This Policy
Because FlowFI is designed as a secure, local scientific tool, our data practices are unlikely to change. If any future updates introduce optional network features (such as automated version checking or crash reporting), this policy will be updated accordingly, and any data transmission will remain strictly opt-in.

## 4. Contact
If you have any questions regarding this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's repository: [https://github.com/jameswilsenach/FlowFI]
